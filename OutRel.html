Akkalkot Swami Samarth

The Akkalkot Swami Samarth Maharaj Temple is one of the main attractions in the city of Solapur. It is dedicated to Swami Samarth Maharaj, a 19th century saint, who is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya. The temple sees thousands of devotees every year.

Iskcon Temple

This iskcon temple commemorates Lord Chaitanya's actual recorded visit to pandharpur in search of his brother. It reminds one of the reach of Krishna bhakti across India. The temple environment is serene and blissful with many trees around, beautifully decorated deities of lord Krishna and Radha devi
