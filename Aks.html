Akkalkot Swami Samarth

The Akkalkot Swami Samarth Maharaj Temple is one of the main attractions in the city of Solapur. It is dedicated to Swami Samarth Maharaj, a 19th century saint, who is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya. The temple sees thousands of devotees every year. The temple complex, also known as the Vatavruksh Mandir, houses an ancient Banyan tree. This is where the saint would preach to his followers. The tree is the main attraction in the temple today.
