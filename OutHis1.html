Dhotri Fort

The route from Dhotri village circumambulates a huge bastion and takes us to the main entrance of the fort. The main doorway is 12 ft tall facing northwards. There are porches built inside the doorway and the fortification wall near the entrance has a terrace room.

Machnur Fort

Mughal emperor Aurangzeb had been facing the wrath of the Marathas for quite a long time. It was because of this he built this fort with the main aim of protection from enemies. This fort which was built in 1615 AD had stationed a huge battalion of the Mughal force. Aurangzeb had hosted his court matters in this very fort.

Mangalwedha

The current structure of the fort is a small miniature fort type with four bastions and houses the office of Divisional Magistrate.At the corner we can find some sculptures and idols of Gods and Goddesses. The present ruined state of the fort hints that the fort must have been huge in the earlier times.
