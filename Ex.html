Welcome To PICNIC POINT - An Agri Tourism Center

Picnic Point is an Agri-Tourism Center where tourists come for picnics to recreate and relax themselves.

Best One Day Picnic Spot Near Solapur - Where exclusive is always inclusive

At Picnic Point – An Agri-Tourism Center, People gather to ‘Get Together’, ‘Wet Together’ and ‘Eat Together’ ! Picnic Point has been registered under both MART and ATDC, the Heads of Agri -Tourism Concept. It is located on the bank of canal, near Solapur-Pune National Express Highway No. 65 at the distance of just 15 minutes from Solapur.

Tourists recreate and relax themselves at the exotic picnic spot near Solapur. With wide range of food festivals like Maharashtrian Festival, Mango Festival, Special Shravan – DaalBati Churma Festival, Hurda (Sorghuam) Festival, guests enjoy the Limca Book Record Winner, Zoola Bhojan – The Swing and Eat Concept.
